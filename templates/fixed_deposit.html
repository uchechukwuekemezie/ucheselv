Explore Our Fixed Deposit Options

Welcome to our Fixed Deposit services, where we offer you a variety of options to grow your savings securely. Fixed Deposits are an ideal choice if you're looking for a risk-free investment with stable returns.

Our Fixed Deposit Plans

  • Short-Term Deposits: Perfect for those seeking quick returns. Tenures range from 30 days to 6 months with attractive interest rates.
  • Medium-Term Deposits: Ideal for savers with a mid-range investment horizon. Enjoy higher interest rates for tenures of 1 to 3 years.
  • Long-Term Deposits: Best suited for long-term financial goals. Secure the highest interest rates for tenures extending beyond 3 years.

Our Fixed Deposit accounts come with features like auto-renewal options, loan facilities against deposits, and flexible interest payouts. Tailor your deposit plan as per your convenience and financial goals.

Not sure which plan is right for you? Our Savings Growth Calculator can help you estimate your potential earnings. For more personalized guidance, our team of dedicated financial advisors is here to assist you.
